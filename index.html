Direct Cool or Frost-free Cooling tech

Frost-free refrigerators do not require any manual de-icing or defrosting. It is handled automatically. Direct cool refrigerators can keep your food cool and fresh for a longer duration in case of a power outage.

Energy Star Rating

Every refrigerator comes with Energy Efficiency Ratings (EER) that are standardized by the Bureau of Energy Efficiency (BEE). The more the number of stars for a fridge, the higher the energy efficiency it has. A 5 Star fridge will consume approximately 10-15% less power than a 3 Star fridge for a similar run.
